About Yaya

Yaya Starchild writes gentle, resonant poems that trace the quiet magic in ordinary lives. Her voice is for dreamers, and especially tender to women of color who hold multitudes.

Journey

Growing up with star charts and storybooks, Yaya learned to listen for light. 'Suncatcher Spirit' collects years of small poems that practice hope.

Appearances

Yaya reads at community centers, libraries, and seasonal festivals. Subscribe for upcoming events.
